— The 6-Step WDF Process —

Drop-Off → Done · Every Order

INTAKE · weigh and ticket
Customer drops bag. Weigh on commercial scale. Enter customer details into Cents. Print barcode ticket. Note any special instructions: bleach-safe, hang-dry, separate whites, allergies. Tag bag with ticket.
SORT · whites, colors, delicates
Quick triage. Anything with a stain pre-treat. Anything delicate goes in a mesh bag. Match every load to the right machine and water temp.
WASH · log start time in Cents
Hit start in Cents — system tracks the order's location and time. Customer can check status in the app. Use the right detergent (member orders get the premium scent option).
DRY · monitor, don't over-dry
Pull when ~95% dry — that final 5% finishes during folding and prevents wrinkles. Over-drying damages fabric and burns power.
FOLD · the SPINS standard
Shirts: thirded, sleeves in, collar up. Pants: folded long, then in half. Towels: thirds. Underwear & socks: bundled in 6s. Folded clothes go in clean SPINS branded bag with ticket on top.
NOTIFY · automated text via Cents
"Your SPINS WDF order is ready! Pick up by 8 PM tonight or we'll deliver tomorrow." Customer comes in. Verify ticket number. Hand off. Smile. Pitch membership if they're not already a member.

✓ Always Do This

  • Weigh every bag — never eyeball
  • Pre-treat visible stains
  • Use a mesh bag for delicates
  • Fold the SPINS standard, every time
  • Tag the bag immediately
  • Notify via Cents the moment it's ready

✕ Never Do This

  • Mix two customers' loads — ever
  • Skip the ticket scan
  • Over-dry · damages fabric
  • Use bleach without specific note
  • Fold sloppy when busy
  • Send the bag out without verifying ticket
